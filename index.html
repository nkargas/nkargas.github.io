About me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. In 2013 I earned the Diploma of Engineering at the School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Techinical University of Crete, Greece. During the last year of my studies I was member of the RoboCup team Kouretes and completed my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Associate Professor M. G. Lagoudakis. In 2015 I received my Master of Science degree at the same department under the supervision of Associate Professor Aggelos Bletsas. Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I am working with the Signal and Tensor Analytics Research (STAR) group under the supervision of Professor Nikos Sidiropoulos.
