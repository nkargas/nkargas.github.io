About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, at University of Minnesota advised by Professor Nikolaos D. Sidiropoulos. I received the Diploma and M.Sc. degrees from the School of Electronic & Computer Engineering at the Technical University of Crete.

My research interests lie in the areas of Machine Learning, Statistics and Optimization. Recently, I have worked on latent variable models with identifiability guarantees, crowdsourcing and system identification.
